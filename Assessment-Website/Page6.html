Nuuk (Danish: Godthaab or Godthåb) is the capital of Greenland, located on the west coast of the island in the Capital Region!


Nuuk is the home of the Nuuk Cultural Center, Ilisimatusarfik University, and northern lights nearly every night!

Despite being the capital of Greenland, Nuuk isn't snowy year round. But when it does snow, it is certainly a beautiful sight to see.

Nuuk translates roughly to "Cape" or "Headland"

In the summer, the beautiful nature of the country is unveiled, and you can see the mountains and grass.

Naalakkersuisut, the Government of Greenland, is located in Nuuk

They regularly hold meetings to discuss what they can do for Greenland
